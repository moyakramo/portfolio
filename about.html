About

From finance
to AI & ML.

I'm Moya Kramo, a graduate student with a background in finance and a focus on data science and AI. I sit at the intersection of two worlds, financial markets and investments and the fast-moving frontier of machine learning, and I use that vantage point to make AI legible to the people who have to make decisions about it, and to help them leverage and capitalize on it.

What I do

Translation, not hype

The AI conversation is loud, and most of the noise isn't useful. My aim is to strip that back: to explain what a technique actually does, where it genuinely changes a decision, and where it doesn't, so leaders can act on it.

Finance

Domain grounding

I understand markets, risk, and how capital-allocation decisions actually get made under uncertainty.

AI / ML

Technical fluency

Graduate training in data science and AI, enough depth to separate real capability from marketing.

Bridge

From insight to tools

I turn what I learn into tools, strategies, and resources people can use to capitalize on the rise of AI.

Value proposition

Why it matters

I engage with AI enthusiasts, finance professionals, founders, and strategy leaders to show how AI can benefit them and how they can leverage it, translating complex, technical ideas into clear, actionable insight, without hype or oversimplification.
